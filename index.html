Welcome to Immanuel Christian Fellowship!


"Him we proclaim, warning everyone and teaching everyone with all wisdom, that we may present everyone mature in Christ." Colossians 1:28 (ESV)


Immanuel Christian Fellowship (ICF) is the college ministry of Immanuel Bible Church. Our desire is to connect college students to the local church, proclaiming Christ to them with a high view of God and of Scripture, so that each one would be mature in Christ. Our aim is to equip these students to serve Christ and His church, and to evangelize students who are lost without Christ.

Church Rides

We provide rides to Immanuel Bible Church every Sunday. You can sign up for a ride here if you want to come to church (sign up by Friday night)! A typical Sunday service is from 9am to 10:30am, with an Equip Hour (more teaching) from 11am to 12pm.
