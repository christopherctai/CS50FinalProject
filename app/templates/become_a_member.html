WHY MEMBERSHIP?

At Immanuel Bible Church, we believe that membership is a critical component of Christian faithfulness. Even if you're just here for 4, 2, or 1 year as a college student, membership is a healthy way to connect with the local body of Christ. With membership, we commit to upholding the church's ministry, submitting to church leadership, practicing the one-another commands in the Bible, and supporting the church's worship, ordinances, disciplines, and doctrines.
